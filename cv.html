Artistic Focus

My work explores the blurred boundaries between identity, agency, and community in our hyperconnected age. I create digital worlds that oscillate between the hyperactive and the meditative, examining how digital design can subtly shape behavior and perception.

Influenced by posthumanist thought, I see technology as an extension of the body: a merging of biology and code that challenges our understanding of embodiment. My visual experiments serve as both personal reflections and broader social commentaries.
