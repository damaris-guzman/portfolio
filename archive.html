Out of Sync

Out of Sync is a found footage project exploring the human mind's relationship with technology and chaos. Through a mix of my own 3D animations, free vectors, and early web footage, it portrays the overwhelming nature of modern life. A moment of silence leads to a reconnecting with nature, symbolizing presence amidst chaos.

The project reflects on technology's impact on the psyche, urging viewers to embrace the serenity of the present moment.
