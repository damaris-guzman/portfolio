Served as Associate Producer on VPM3D v5.0 - Free Throw, a live projection mapping experience that transforms the east façade of the SAP Center into a luminous arena for civic play. Conceived by G. Craig Hobbs and powered by Art 175 students alongside graduates and alumni from San José State University, the project reimagined downtown San José's Barack Obama Boulevard as an immersive interactive digital canvas across March 26 and 28, 2026.

I facilitated weekly Zoom and in person production meetings to align with the lead artist and project stakeholders. I also led the social media strategy from planning through execution, shot and edited photo and video across test shoots and the live event, maintained timelines equipment rosters and call sheets, and supported aesthetic and technical asset reviews.

Partners included City of San José Office of Cultural Affairs, San José Sports Authority and CADRE Media Lab.
